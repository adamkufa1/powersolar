The process
1

Use the calendar on this page

Always book through the calendar below — it's connected to the correct pipeline and triggers WhatsApp reminders automatically.

2

Enter the lead's details

Fill in their name, email, and phone number. The system uses the phone number to send their WhatsApp reminder.

⚠️ Double-check the phone number — that's where the WhatsApp reminder gets sent.
3

Pick a time slot together

While you have them on the phone, choose the slot together in real time.

4

Let them know what to expect

Tell them: "You'll receive a WhatsApp reminder before your appointment."
